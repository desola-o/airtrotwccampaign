Travel to the United States: The "FIFA PASS"

If you are traveling to the U.S. and require a visitor visa (B1/B2), you may be eligible for the FIFA PASS. This is an expedited system for ticket holders to secure priority visa interviews.

Canada & Mexico: Border-Crossing Simplified

Canada

eTA vs. Visitor Visa

  • eTA: For visa-exempt nationals arriving by air. Electronically linked to your passport.
  • Visitor Visa: Required for most other travelers.

Traveling with Minors?

Canada has strict rules for travelers under 18. We ensure you have the correct notarized authorizations ready.

Mexico: 180 Days of Football
